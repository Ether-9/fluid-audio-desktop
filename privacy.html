Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Fluid Audio is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application and website.

2. Data We Collect

  • No Personal Data: Fluid Audio does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • Local Audio Files: All audio files you load are processed entirely on your device. They are never uploaded to any server.
  • License Keys: If you purchase a license, your key is validated through Lemon Squeezy (our payment processor). We do not store your license keys.
  • Analytics: We do not use analytics trackers, cookies, or telemetry.

3. How We Use Your Data

Since we don't collect any data, we don't use any data. Your audio files stay on your computer. Your license validation is handled securely by Lemon Squeezy.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Lemon Squeezy: Used for payment processing and license validation. Their privacy policy applies to any payment information you provide.
  • GitHub: We use GitHub to host our code and releases. No user data is transmitted to GitHub through our app.

5. Data Security

All processing is performed locally on your device. We don't transmit, store, or process any of your data on our servers.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
